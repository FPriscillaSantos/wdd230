Demography

In 2022, the municipality's population was counted by the Brazilian Institute of Geography and Statistics (IBGE) at 698,642 inhabitants, being the eighth most populous in the state and the most populous in the Ribeirão Preto Mesoregion, with a population density of 1072.73 inhabitants. per km², with an average of 2.61 residents per household. Women are the majority, representing 52.43% (366,324) of residents, against 47.57% (332,318) of men, a difference of 34,006.
The Municipal Human Development Index (HDI-M) of Ribeirão Preto is considered high by the United Nations Development Program (UNDP). Its value in 2010 was 0.800, being the sixth highest in the entire state of São Paulo (in 645 municipalities); the eighth in the entire Southeast Region of Brazil (in 1666) and the 40th in all of Brazil (out of 5507). The city has the majority of indicators that are high and above those of the national average according to the UNDP.
